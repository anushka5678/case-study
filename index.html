Case Study on Google Lens and MobileNet Model

I have tested 3 everyday items to see whether Google Lens or MobileNet Model can identify them better.

1. Perfume Bottle

MobileNet Model- MobileNet Model recognised it as "Hair Spray".

Google Lens- Google Lens detected it as "Japanese Cherry Blossom Fragrance Mist Bath and Body Works"

In this case, Google Lens was more accurate.

2. Paint Brush

MobileNet Model- MobileNet Model recognised it as "Paint Brush".

Google Lens- Google Lens detected it as "Brush"

In this case,MobileNet Model was more accurate.

3. Mobile Phone

MobileNet Model- MobileNet Model recognised it as "Cell phone".

Google Lens- Google Lens detected it as "Samsung Galaxy J2 Prime phone"

In this case,Google Lens was more accurate.

I have tested 3 items. Google Lens got 2/3 accurate. MobileNet Model got 1/3 accurate. I believe, according to this case study, Google Lens was more accurate than MobileNet Model as it succeeded in perfectly guessing most of the items.
